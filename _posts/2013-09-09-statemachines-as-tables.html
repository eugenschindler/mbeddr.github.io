--- layout: blogpost title: New mbeddr Release --- The release of MPS 3.0 is imminent, and one of the new features of MPS 3.0 is the ability to have several independently defined editors for the same concepts. In addition, there is also a new add-on developed by Sascha Lisson that can do really nice tables. We have used these two features in mbeddr to support editing tables using a tabular notation.
