--- layout: blogpost title: Eclipse Proposal for mbeddr available --- We may have mentioned this before, but we are in the process of making mbeddr an Eclipse project. We have been working on the proposal for a while, and it is now online at eclipse.org. So if you are interested in using or participating in the development of mbeddr, there will soon be a well-defined governance process. If you are interested in the project, why don't you register as an interested party for the project?
